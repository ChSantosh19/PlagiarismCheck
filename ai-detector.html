How It Works

Our AI text detector analyzes linguistic patterns and statistical features that often differentiate between human and AI-generated content:

Repetitive Patterns

AI often generates text with detectable repetitive structures and predictable patterns.

Vocabulary Diversity

Our algorithm measures word variation and compares it to typical human writing patterns.

Filler Words

Human writing typically contains more filler words and verbal tics than AI-generated content.

Sentence Variance

We analyze statistical variance in sentence length, which differs between human and AI authors.

Personal Anecdotes

Human writers tend to include more personal experiences and first-person pronouns.

Formality Structure

We detect overly formal language structures that are common in AI-generated text.
